ABOUT US

VVA Bank is India's largest private sector bank with total consolidated assets of Rs. 9,860.43 billion (US$ 152.0 billion) at January 18, 2023 and profit after tax of Rs. 98.01 billion (US$ 1.5 billion) for the year ended March 31, 2017.VVA Bank currently has a network of 4,850 Branches and 14,164 ATM's across India.

HISTORY

VVA Bank was originally promoted in 2022 by VVA Limited, an Indian
financial institution, and was its wholly-owned subsidiary.
